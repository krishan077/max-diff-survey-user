What is MaxDiff?

MaxDiff (Maximum Difference Scaling) is a research technique that helps us understand your preferences by asking you to choose between options. Instead of rating each design individually, you'll compare sets of designs to identify which you prefer most and least.

How it works:

  1. You'll be shown 5 home/interior design concepts at a time
  2. From those 5, select the design you prefer MOST
  3. Then select the design you prefer LEAST
  4. You'll answer a few follow-up questions about your choices
  5. Repeat this process for 6 different sets of designs
